# Privacy Policy — readThings

## Data Collection
readThings does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Camera
The camera is used solely for real-time object detection on your device. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded.

## On-Device Processing
All object detection (ML Kit) and translation (Gemma 4 E2B) run entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone.

## Storage
- Photos are stored in the app's local document directory
- Vocabulary items and translations are stored in a local SQLite database
- Settings are stored locally via AsyncStorage

## Third-Party Services
readThings does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Model Download
The translation model (Gemma 4 E2B, ~1.3GB) is downloaded from Hugging Face once. After download, all translation processing happens offline.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
